What We Do

How We Achieve Our Goals

Mentorship

Mentoring students by teaching them and providing opportunities to gain experience in relevant industries.

Solutions

Creating innovative tech solutions for charitable organizations who are hindered by the lack of adequate technology.

Empower

Motivate students and the surrounding community to give them more power through technology.

Mission Statement

We aim to assist underprivileged communities by
helping them become independent through the leverage of technology
and enable students to become more prepared upon entering the workforce.

Helen Hu

Helen Hu

VP Finance

Oh hi there! I see you’ve stumbled upon my little page. My name is Helen Hu and I am the VP of Finance at DevFound! I am in charge of managing the finances of the chapter as well as securing clients- basically finding us jobs to keep our developers busy. Currently a second year student at Rotman Commerce, I am pursuing a specialization in Public Accounting and wishes to work as an auditor upon graduation. Developers Foundation is a really great organization that strives to help disadvantaged charity organizations stand up on their feet. Through having an informative and working website, charitable organizations are able to promote themselves and fulfill their initiatives better. I strongly resonate with DevFound’s mission and purpose, and I hope that you will too! Being involved in Developer’s Foundation has exposed me to a whole other side of the world, and it truly is a great way to sharpen your web designing skills and obtain mentorship along the way. Whatever your future career goal is, DevFound definitely has a role for you :)!
